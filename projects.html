Projects & Research

Currently, Grup 2 is working exclusively on the CanSat project. However, throughout its history, Grup 2 has taken part in many other related undertakings.

Past projects: KubeSat

Our team at Grup 2 is no stranger to space missions involving satellites for measuring high-altitude data. Between September and October 2022, members of Grup 2 participated in the KubeSat project, organised by the UIB and IBSTEAM.

Quite similarly to CanSat, the KubeSat project aimed to measure environmental data (temperature, pressure, humidity, gas concentration) in the lower atmosphere. In that sense, the final working satellite was slowly elevated to 30 km altitude using a weather balloon, from where it initiated its descent towards the recovery team.

This endeavour received ample attention from the regional Balearic press, including Ultima Hora, public broadcaster IB3, and others.

CanSat

The CanSat project is our most recent undertaking, which is structured into two separate phases:

  • Primary mission: this mission is common to all CanSat participants, and involves the following:
    1. Launching the satellite via a specially provided rocket.
    2. Measuring basic environmental variables (temperature, pressure, altitude) and transmitting it real-time over a radio communication link.
    3. Safely landing and recovering the satellite (by using parachutes to decelerate).
  • Secondary mission: measuring earth surface radiation in the infrared spectrum to detect specific patterns. For more meaningful analysis, this data will be linked to position and orientation data (provided by on-board GPS and accelerometer modules).
