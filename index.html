Grup 2

Grup 2 is committed to advancing autonomous spaceflight knowledge.

We are an aeronautics organisation dedicated to research in advanced spaceflight missions, including autonomous launch and recovery systems.

About us and our mission

Grup 2, integrated by four seventeen year-old students from Binissalem, Mallorca, has been created with the aim to achieve the CanSat project goals, endorsed by the ESA (European Space Agency). Looking forward to developing an efficient prototype, our main interest is learning during the procedure. From coding a suitable structure, to designing and constructing a functional design, every piece of work has been precisely calculated for an adequate suiting to the proposed conditions.

Within this project, two important clauses are to be fulfilled. Two different missions, two distinct projects: both in a can-sized structure, and yet both willing to impress.

Current progress on the project

The CanSat project is currently work in progress. As we believe in transparency, we will share all of our advances on the projects section and on our various social media accounts.

We are actively working on finishing and refining our first prototype, which will be presented and assessed at the campus of the University of the Balearic Islands in March.

Technical details

We have drafted a preliminary report, in which we describe an outline of our both our future and past work. This report serves as general guidance for the project, helping us to carefully plan and execute the next steps.
